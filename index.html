Turn Glucose Management Into a Game

Glucose Smash connects to your LibreLinkUp account to help you track your glucose levels in a fun, motivating way. Earn achievements, track streaks, and celebrate your successes.

Real-Time Tracking

Connect to LibreLinkUp to sync your glucose readings automatically. View your current glucose level, trends, and history at a glance.

Achievement System

Unlock achievements as you reach your glucose goals. Track perfect days, maintain streaks, and celebrate milestones with fun reward icons.

Beautiful Visualizations

See your glucose data in intuitive graphs and charts. Track your time in range, average glucose, and daily statistics with easy-to-read visuals.

Goal Setting

Set personalized goals for your average glucose and time in range. Get instant feedback on whether you're meeting your targets.

Streak Tracking

Build and maintain streaks for perfect days, average glucose goals, and time in range targets. Watch your longest streak grow over time.

iPad Optimized

Enjoy a beautiful layout optimized for both iPhone and iPad. View more data at once on the larger iPad screen.
